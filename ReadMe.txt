--- CONTROLS ---
ESC - Pause/Options Panel
WASD - Movement
Hold left mouse - Put out bush fires (if you have water)

--- GOAL ---
Put out all the fires in the time limit
You don't have lives" but dying (drowning, getting too close to fire) does kill time

--- EXTRA FEATURES ---
Pause panel
Gameover/Next Level animations
Randomly selecting which bush to put on fire
Fire particle system
Waterhose particle system
Animations/particles on robot & waterpacks (bouncing and rings coming from bottom)
Robot (I made it in blender)
